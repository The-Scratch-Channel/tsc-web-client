# DevJokes

## What are DevJokes?
DevJokes are easter eggs/secrets that are publicly available on the TSC repo and site. These are for laughs or just not knowing what to work on but wanting to work on something.
## Rules of DevJokes
Please do not include any of the following:
* 1. Heavy swearing (Minor swears like damn or hell may be allowed)
  2. Racism or racial slurs
  3. Sexism. (Respect others no matter sexuality or gender!)
  4. NSFW
